About Me

I am currently a PhD student at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), supervised by Prof. Yang Gao and affiliated with the Centre for AI Robotics in Space Sustainability.

Previously, I completed my MRes in Healthcare Technologies at King's College London, supervised by Dr. Erika Molteni, Dr. Michela Antonelli and Dr. Thomas Booth. I obtained my BEng degree at South China University of Technology, majoring in Process Equipment and Control Engineering with a minor in Computer Science, supervised by Prof. Dong Zhang.

Beyond robotics, I am also a photography enthusiast and home cook. Click here to view some of my photography work.

Projects

A Toolkit For Tele-Operated Robotic Thrombectomy in Acute Stroke

Developed automated navigation for mechanical thrombectomy using PPO in the SOFA framework. Designed experiments comparing algorithm performance with human operation. Article expected to be published in 2025.

Paper Publication in Preparation

Multimodal Deep Learning for Mental Stress State and Loneliness Detection

Developed Transformer-based models for mental stress and loneliness detection with variable modal signal inputs. Explored modality loss effects and robustness enhancement methods.
