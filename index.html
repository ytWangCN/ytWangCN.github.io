Bio

Yitong has just got his Bachelor of Engineering at School of Mechanical and Automotive Engineering  SCUT, with a minor in Computer Science. His research interests include Robotics, Control, Perception and Machine Learning.

He used to be a member of RobotLab at South China University of Technology. He is also the Program Leader of Robot Competition Team at Season 2020-2021. Besides, he is also very interested in photography, click here to view some of his photographic works.
